Who is Crony made by?

Crony is a web application made by Lennox Anderson and Roger Wang. with our mentor being Aayush Saxena

Why did we make Crony?

Although hiking and backpacking can be foreseen as a great outdoor activity or hobby, there is a lot of risk when doing it alone. In addition, everything is more fun when you're hiking with a friend.

How does Crony work?

Crony works by using your location to find nearby users as well as hiking trails that fit your individual level in hopes of you going on a hike with a new friend.
